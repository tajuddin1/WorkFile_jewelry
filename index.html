Complete Jewelry Retail Software

A full-featured solution for sales, inventory, repairs, CRM, accounting, and more.

Point Of Sale (POS)

  • Manage purchases, returns, repairs, and appraisals
  • Collect customer info (address, phone, email)
  • Easily generate sales tax reports

Inventory

  • Track inventory/transfers for multiple stores
  • Automatically reorder low inventory items
  • Monitor items/inventory in the vendor portal

Repair Orders

  • Keep track of customer repairs
  • Take photos of repair items
  • Create job bags and track from store to shop

CRM

  • Set reminders for customers
  • Link related accounts (spouse, children)

Accounting

  • Easily integrate with QuickBooks

Jewelry Software for Manufacturers and Wholesalers!

We provide jewelry software for manufacturers, wholesalers, retailers and refineries. Our packages include accounting (memo and invoice), inventory (jewelry and diamond), tagging, analysis, manufacturing, imaging, vendor consignments and accounts payable. Export to QuickBooks, automatic update of your website, EDI, RFID, automated Fedex, UPS, ParcelPro, MalcaAmit label printing and tracking are available. Based on latest C#/.Net technology. Unlimited training is included with our products at no cost. Automating jewelry business since 1987.
